Project J

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2026  |  Last Updated: June 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Justin Harmke ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Project J mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using Project J, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address (or an Apple-generated private relay address). This is used solely to identify your account and sync your data securely.

Profile and Physical Data

  • Name, date of birth, and biological sex
  • Current weight, goal weight, and weight loss / maintenance pace
  • Height and body measurements
  • Activity level and training frequency
  • Fitness goals and calorie targets

Nutrition and Food Data

  • Food log entries including food names, serving sizes, and nutritional information (calories, macronutrients, fiber, sodium, cholesterol, saturated fat)
  • Meal timing and meal slot assignments
  • Daily water intake
  • Custom foods and recipes you create
  • Food favorites and recent searches
  • Barcode-to-food assignments

Workout and Activity Data

  • Exercise logs including exercise name, sets, reps, weight, duration, and cardio details
  • Workout programs, weekly templates, and training tags
  • Workout notes and effort ratings
  • Daily workout completion status

Sleep Data

  • Sleep duration, bedtime, and wake time (entered manually or sourced from Apple HealthKit)
  • Sleep stage data (Core, Deep, REM, Awake) when provided by Apple HealthKit
  • Subjective sleep feel ratings

Journal and Reflection Data

  • Personal journal entries, including workout notes, daily thoughts, and gratitude entries
  • Bible verse reflections and annotations
  • Gratitude streaks and log history
  • Bible reading history, bookmarked verses, and reader settings

Prayer Requests (Optional)

If you choose to submit a prayer request through the App, your message, display name, and email address (if associated with your account) are sent directly to the developer via email and stored securely in our database under your user ID. Submitting a prayer request is entirely voluntary. Prayer requests are used solely to personally receive and respond to your request — they are never shared with third parties.

Faith and Wellness Preferences

  • Faith Journey setting (Rooted, Exploring, or Not Right Now) — a personal preference that determines which faith features are active
  • Coaching Style setting (Discipline, Balanced, or Mindful)

App Settings and Preferences

  • Visual theme, accent color, and display preferences
  • Card layout order and visibility settings
  • HealthKit connection status
  • Burn accuracy adjustment and other calculation preferences
  • Intermittent fasting settings and window history
  • Notification preferences and reminder times (stored on-device only, never transmitted to our servers)

Health Data from Apple HealthKit

With your explicit permission through iOS, we read the following data from Apple HealthKit:

  • Step count
  • Walking and running distance
  • Active energy burned and resting (basal) energy burned
  • Flights climbed and stand hours
  • Exercise time and mindful minutes
  • Sleep analysis (including sleep stages and times)
  • Heart rate (including readings during sleep and workouts)
  • Resting heart rate
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Respiratory rate
  • Blood oxygen saturation (SpO2)
  • VO2 Max (cardio fitness)
  • Cardio fitness recovery
  • Body weight, body fat percentage, and waist circumference
  • Basal body temperature
  • Workout history (for import into the App)

You control HealthKit permissions at any time through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > Project J.

Microphone and Voice Input (Optional)

Project J offers an optional voice dictation feature so you can speak a food or meal description instead of typing it. If you use this feature, the App requests microphone access and uses speech recognition to convert your speech into text. Depending on your device and iOS settings, speech recognition may be processed on your device or by Apple's speech recognition service. Audio is captured only while you are actively dictating, is used solely to fill in the text field you are editing, and is never stored or transmitted by us to our servers. The microphone is never accessed in the background or without your action.

3. Apple HealthKit — Special Notice

Project J's use of Apple HealthKit data is governed by strict Apple guidelines and our firm commitments:

  • HealthKit data is used only to provide health and fitness features within the App. It is not used for any other purpose.
  • HealthKit data is never used for advertising, ad targeting, or marketing of any kind.
  • HealthKit data is never shared with third parties for any purpose that is incompatible with providing health and fitness management services to you.
  • HealthKit data is never sold to data brokers, insurers, employers, financial institutions, or any other entity.
  • HealthKit data is never used to determine insurance eligibility, employment status, or for any discriminatory purpose.
  • Health metrics read from HealthKit are displayed to you within the App and stored in your personal cloud account (Firebase Firestore), accessible only under your authenticated user ID.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide, maintain, and improve the App's features:

  • To calculate personalized calorie targets, macronutrient goals, and fitness metrics
  • To track your progress against your personal health and fitness goals
  • To display trends, history, streaks, and insights within the App
  • To sync your data securely across your devices via Firebase
  • To authenticate your identity and protect your account
  • To provide food nutritional information through the FatSecret food database
  • To generate personalized reports and analysis within the App
  • To personally receive and read prayer requests you voluntarily submit through the App
  • To schedule local notifications on your device for reminders about goals, streaks, and fasting windows — all notifications are generated on-device and no notification content is transmitted to our servers
  • To let you dictate food and meal descriptions by voice using your device's microphone and speech recognition, when you choose to use that feature
  • To generate personalized coaching tips using an AI service (Anthropic). When the Smart Coach feature runs, a summary of your recent health and fitness data (such as calorie totals, macronutrient averages, weight trend, sleep scores, and activity levels) is transmitted to Anthropic's API to generate a coaching observation. No personally identifying information such as your name, email, or account ID is included in this transmission. The data sent is limited to the aggregate metrics needed to generate the tip and is not used to train Anthropic's models.
  • To power the Faith AI Companion (Halo). When you send a message to Halo, your typed message and recent conversation history are transmitted to Anthropic's API to generate a response. Your name, email, account ID, prayer log entries, and journal content are never included. Conversation content is not used to train Anthropic's models and is not logged or stored by us beyond what is needed to maintain your active session.
  • To store food photos you attach to food entries in Firebase Storage, so they are preserved across devices and after reinstalling the App. Photos are stored under your authenticated user ID and are deleted when you delete the associated food entry or your account.

We do not use your information for advertising, behavioral profiling, or any purpose outside of operating the App's core features.

5. Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google LLC)

We use Firebase Authentication to manage your account, Firebase Firestore to securely sync your data, and Firebase Storage to store food photos you attach to food entries. Your data is stored under your unique user ID and governed by Firebase's security rules, ensuring only your authenticated account can read or write your data. Google's privacy policy applies to Firebase: https://policies.google.com/privacy

FatSecret

Food search functionality is powered by the FatSecret food database. When you search for food, your search query is transmitted to FatSecret's API to retrieve nutritional data. Search queries are not linked to your personal identity in our systems. This product uses the FatSecret Platform API but is not endorsed or certified by FatSecret. FatSecret's privacy policy: https://www.fatsecret.com/privacy.aspx

Apple Sign-In

If you authenticate using Sign in with Apple, Apple provides your name and email address (or a private relay email). Apple's privacy policy governs this transaction: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Google Sign-In

If you authenticate using Google Sign-In, Google provides your name and email address. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Anthropic (AI Coaching, Meal Estimation & Faith Companion)

The Smart Coach feature uses Anthropic's Claude AI to phrase personalized coaching tips. When the Smart Coach runs, a structured summary of your recent health and fitness metrics (such as calorie averages, macronutrient totals, weight trend, sleep scores, and activity levels over a recent window of days) is transmitted to Anthropic's API. No personally identifying information is included in this transmission — your name, email, account ID, journal entries, and prayer content are never sent to Anthropic. The data transmitted is limited to the aggregate metrics required to generate the coaching tip and is not used to train Anthropic's models. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to data processed through their API: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

The AI Meal Estimator feature also uses Anthropic's Claude AI. When you request a meal estimate, the meal photo you choose to attach (if any) and the text description you provide are transmitted to Anthropic's API solely to generate the nutritional estimate you see. This data is sent only when you actively request an estimate, is used only to produce that estimate, and is not used to train Anthropic's models. We recommend you do not include personally identifying information in your meal descriptions. Anthropic's privacy policy linked above applies to this data.

The Faith AI Companion (Halo) also uses Anthropic's Claude AI. When you send a message to Halo, your typed message and recent conversation history (up to the last several exchanges) are transmitted to Anthropic's API to generate a response. Your name, email address, account ID, prayer log entries, and journal content are never transmitted. Conversation content is not used to train Anthropic's models. A server-side crisis detection layer screens messages before they reach the AI model; messages identified as indicating a crisis trigger a hardcoded response with mental health resources rather than an AI reply.

We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or any other third-party data processors beyond those listed above.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in the following locations:

  • On your device: Using iOS local storage (AsyncStorage). This data is sandboxed to the App and is not accessible to other apps or parties.
  • Firebase Firestore (cloud): Your health, fitness, and preference data is mirrored to Firebase Firestore under your unique user ID. Firebase security rules ensure only requests authenticated with your specific account credentials can read or write your data.
  • Firebase Storage (cloud): Food photos you attach to food entries are stored in Firebase Storage under your unique user ID. This allows photos to be preserved across devices and after reinstalling the App. Firebase security rules restrict access to your authenticated account only.

We implement appropriate technical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data We Do Not Collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Device location or GPS coordinates
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Device fingerprints or unique device identifiers beyond what Firebase Auth requires
  • Behavioral analytics, crash reports, or usage telemetry (no analytics SDK is used)
  • Financial or payment information (the App is free)
  • Contacts, calendar, or other device data
  • Camera access is used for barcode scanning, which is processed on-device and not transmitted. Food photos you attach to food entries are uploaded to Firebase Storage under your account (see Section 6). Meal photos you attach to the AI Meal Estimator are transmitted to Anthropic to generate the nutritional estimate (see Section 5). We never access your camera or photos in the background or without your action.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for their own purposes.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • Open the App and navigate to Settings > Account > Delete Account
  • Confirm deletion through the in-app prompts

When you delete your account:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account is permanently deleted
  • All cloud-synced data in Firebase Firestore (users/[your ID]) is permanently erased, including prayer requests
  • All food photos stored in Firebase Storage under your account are permanently deleted
  • All locally stored data on your device is removed
  • Your Apple Sign-In authorization token is revoked with Apple's servers
  • All food logs, workout data, journal entries, Bible reflections, and personal data are permanently deleted

This process is irreversible. We do not retain backup copies of deleted accounts.
